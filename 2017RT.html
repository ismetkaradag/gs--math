The Second Romanian-Turkish

Mathematics Colloquium

25-29 October 2017

Galatasaray University, İstanbul, Turkey


in collaboration with

Institute of Mathematics of the Romanian Academy

and

Romanian–Turkish Joint Laboratory of Mathematical Research


Objectives

This is the follow-up meeting of the First Romanian-Turkish Mathematics Colloquium held in Constanta in 2015. The aim is to foster collaboration between the mathematicians from the two countries. There will be talks from various fields within mathematics. Some funding may be available for qualified research students coming from the region.

Special thanks to H.E. Osman Koray Ertaş, the Ambassador of the Republic of Turkey in Romania, for kindly supporting the cooperation between Romanian and Turkish mathematical communities. The Romanian participants are also very appreciative to Romanya Türk Iş Adamlari Derneği, and in particular to Mr. Murat Demiray, for partially supporting their participation.
